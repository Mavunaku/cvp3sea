About the Property

Recently renovated 3-bedroom apartment in the BEST neighborhood of Binghamton. This spacious furnished apartment in the first floor of the building (1800 sq ft.). The apartment with a large living and dining room with a fireplace, a knotty pine den, and a new kitchen with all brand-new appliances. The bedrooms are all a good size, and the master bedroom has a brand-new bathroom. The apartment also has a washer and dryer in the unit. The apartment is located on Grand Blvd in a quiet neighborhood close to BU University, parks, and shopping. It's also close to Main Street as well as to public transportation, so you can easily get around town.

Ideal for graduate students or healthcare professionals.
