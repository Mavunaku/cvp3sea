Explore Ithaca
Outdoors • City

Ithaca, New York

Situated in the Finger Lakes region, home to Cornell University, multi-tiered waterfalls, and stunning gorges.

Visit Park
Outdoors

Taughannock Falls State Park

Features a 215-foot waterfall descending beyond rugged precipices that rise almost 400 feet above the ravine.

Explore Glen
Outdoors

Watkins Glen State Park

Famous for its creek that drops 400 feet in less than two miles, passing 200-foot cliffs and 19 waterfalls.

Discover Syracuse
Culture • City

Syracuse, New York

Home to the Erie Canal Museum, Everson Museum of Art, and the Milton J. Rubenstein Museum of Science & Technology.

Experience Bing
Culture • City

Binghamton, New York

Visit the Ross Park Zoo, wood-carved carousels from the 1920s, and the ornately designed Phelps Mansion Museum.

Visit Cortland

Cortland, New York

Known as the 'Crown City', located in the Southern Tier of New York State and surrounded by beautiful landscapes.
