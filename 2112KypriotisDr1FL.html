About Greek Peak

Greek Peak Mountain Resort is the largest ski resort in Central New York. It has 55 trails, including 44 ski trails, 11 glades, six chair lifts, two surface lifts, a tubing conveyor lift, a beginners' slope, and three Terrain Parks. The resort also has an outdoor adventure center with: Mountain coaster, Zip line tours, Aerial challenge course, Bounce house, Snow tubing.

Hope Lake Lodge is a 3-star hotel located in Cortland, New York. It's part of the Greek Peak Mountain Resort, which is central New York's largest ski resort. The lodge is situated on Hope Lake in the Virgil Valley, with views of the mountains and forests
