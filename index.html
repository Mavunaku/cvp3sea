How It All Began

CVP Properties emerged from a shared vision to create residential spaces tailored to families and busy professionals. With a deep understanding of modern lifestyle demands, we set out to build quality maintained properties that serve as comfortable havens — offering the reliability and care that every resident deserves. Today, CVP Properties stands as a symbol of excellence, delivering unparalleled living experiences in Binghamton and Broome County.

About Binghamton

Residents and visitors alike enjoy hiking and biking in nearby state parks, attending sporting events, and strolling through charming neighborhoods. Binghamton's diverse culinary scene ranges from international cuisines to farm-to-table experiences. With a strong sense of community, Binghamton hosts the Spiedie Fest and Balloon Rally, the LUMA Projection Arts Festival, First Friday Art Walks, and more — ensuring there's always something exciting happening.
